// 01 · About

About Me

I am a Professor in the School of Computer Science & Applications at D Y Patil International University, Akurdi, Pune. With more than 15 years of teaching and research experience, I have worked at premier institutions including KL University, Amity University, MIT ADT University, and Sir Padampat Singhania University.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from VIT University Vellore, and a Post-Doctoral Fellowship from the National Kaohsiung University of Science and Technology, Taiwan, with research focused on Bio-Inspired algorithms for MANETs.

My academic journey is complemented by an MBA with Distinction and active leadership roles including Deputy Dean and NAAC/NBA Core Committee Member. I have guided 3 Ph.D. scholars to successful award of their degrees.
